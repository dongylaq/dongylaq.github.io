About Me

Hello! I am Yulong Dong, a PhD student at University of California, Berkeley. I got my BS in Chemical Physics with honor at University of Science and Technology of China (USTC) in 2018.

My undergraduate research was mainly about soft condensed matter and nonequilibrium statistical mechanics. I did my research under the supervision of Prof. Zhonghuai Hou at USTC. In the summer 2017, I conducted a summer research about multiscale modeling of protein bistability with Prof. Bin Zhang at MIT. After being a graduate student at Berkeley, I started my journey on quantum computation after a discussion with Prof. Lin Lin in Berkeley Math. I am coadvised by Prof. Lin Lin in Math and Prof. Birgitta Whaley in Chemistry. My current research focuses on quantum linear algebra and robust quantum control.
