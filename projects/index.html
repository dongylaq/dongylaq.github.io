    Introduction

    My research interest lies broadly in quantum algorithms, numerical linear algebra, applied and numerical analysis of optimization and control theory. To this point, I have conducted research in the following areas:

    • Quantum linear algebra problems: quantum algorithms for solving numerical linear algebra problems, near-term implementation of quantum algorithms.
    • Robust control optimization in quantum computing: finding optimal control of systems subjecting to uncertain quantum evolution.

    I also remain my interest and curiosity towards stochastic differential equations and applied machine learning, which sprouted since I took several interesting courses in my undergraduate.
